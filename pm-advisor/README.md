# PM Advisor

Get strategic product management advice, prioritization help, and PM coaching.

## What This Skill Does

- Provides strategic PM advice
- Helps with prioritization decisions (ICE/RICE frameworks)
- Coaches on stakeholder management
- Assists with market analysis and positioning
- Gives brutally honest feedback

## How to Use

Open Copilot Chat (`Ctrl+Shift+I`) and use the prompt:

```
#pm [your question or request]
```

### Example Prompts

```
#pm Help me prioritize these 5 features using ICE scoring

#pm How should I handle pushback from engineering on this timeline?

#pm What questions should I ask in customer discovery interviews?

#pm Review this PRD and give me honest feedback

#pm Help me prepare for my stakeholder presentation
```

## What You Get

- Strategic advice based on PM best practices
- Frameworks applied to your specific situation
- Honest feedback (no sugar-coating)
- Actionable next steps

## Tips

- Provide context about your product and market
- Share what you've already tried
- Ask for specific frameworks if you have preferences
- Request follow-up questions for deeper exploration
