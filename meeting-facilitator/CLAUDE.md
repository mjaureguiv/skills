# Meeting Facilitator - Claude Instructions

## Method Selection Process

1. **Understand the goal**: What outcome does the facilitator need?
2. **Assess constraints**: Time, participants, remote/in-person
3. **Recommend method**: Match goal to technique
4. **Provide guide**: Step-by-step instructions

## Method Library

### Ideation Methods

**Brainstorming**
- 10-15 min, any size
- Generate quantity over quality
- No criticism during ideation

**Crazy 8s**
- 8 min, individual then share
- 8 sketches in 8 minutes
- Forces rapid iteration

**SCAMPER**
- 30-45 min
- Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other uses, Eliminate, Reverse

**How Might We**
- 15-20 min
- Reframe problems as opportunities
- "How might we [verb] [user] [need]?"

### Prioritization Methods

**Dot Voting**
- 5-10 min
- Each person gets 3-5 dots
- Vote on favorites

**Impact/Effort Matrix**
- 20-30 min
- Plot items on 2x2 grid
- Quick wins = high impact, low effort

**MoSCoW**
- 30 min
- Must have, Should have, Could have, Won't have

**ICE Scoring**
- 30-45 min
- Impact × Confidence × Ease

### Decision Making

**Fist of Five**
- 2 min per decision
- 5=strong yes, 1=strong no, 3=can live with it

**Consent-based**
- 10-15 min
- "Any objections?" vs voting
- Address only blocking objections

**RAPID**
- Define: Recommend, Agree, Perform, Input, Decide

### Problem Definition

**5 Whys**
- 10-15 min
- Keep asking "why?" to find root cause

**Fishbone Diagram**
- 30 min
- Categories: People, Process, Technology, Environment

**Problem Statement**
- 15 min
- "[User] needs [need] because [insight]"

### Retrospectives

**Start/Stop/Continue**
- 20-30 min
- What should we start, stop, continue doing?

**Sailboat**
- 30-45 min
- Wind (what propels us), Anchors (what holds us back), Rocks (risks)

**4Ls**
- 30 min
- Liked, Learned, Lacked, Longed for
