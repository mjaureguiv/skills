**ANSWER:** Additional information about our DTO proposition
Since last year we have seen our concept of Digital Twins of an Organization evolve to encompass company's systems architecture and business capabilities, in addition to enabling an organization to have a great representation of its current state, operating model, IT construct, and relationship between business processes to best practices. These solutions enable successful digital transformations and operational excellence initiatives especially when complex systems of records are involved in such transformations. We continue to promote the concept of process observability, and now extend this to technology observability across the whole organization, bringing together LOB and IT and delivering complete enterprise observability.

SAP Signavio supports process observability and integrates with SAP LeanIX (technology observability) to deliver end-to-end enterprise observability and make it actionable.

Customers can:
-Build a virtual representation of any process with associated systems , with “to-be” models and “as-is” monitoring
-Model their processes to design optimum flows and maximum efficiency, leveraging best practices, value accelerators and GenAI
-Visualize process adjustments, based on one common collaborative platform
-Simulate different scenarios and calculate the impact of alternative technologies and what this would mean for your as-is IT landscape
-With technology observability identify the opportunity to rationalize and modernize applications whilst mitigating technology risk
-Monitor the way their operations actually work - fact-based and objectively
-Identify effort drivers, reasons for delay, and potential for automation
-Create tangible impact with transparency and continuous process improvements and recommendations
