### ROW 27: SAP Signavio provides extensive process analysis and mining capabilities that allow customers to observe processes and metrics from multiple perspectives (including metadata) also interconnected and aggregated, leveraging benchmarks and best practices if applicable (e.g. the plug and gain best run score cascading from low level scores to one overarching score across areas).

In the latter option, end-to-end workflows with forms-based approval steps and semantic access to business objects, data and services as "actions", task automations (attended and unattended RPA bots) and rule-based decision logic, as well as conditions / branches can be achieved. Additionally, in our prebuilt process analytics we offer best practice-based corrective recommendations, for processes within SAP ERP systems. On a more strategic level, the result from conformance checks is used for deploying process changes to SAP source systems with dedicated governance. Most importantly, for the monitoring part, we provide the option to enhance process models with live operational insights metrics from event logs (e.g. traffic light yellow) and triggers if a metric threshold is met, to support and facilitate collaborative process improvement and redesign based on process mining insights. 
In order to effectively orchestrate process change, SAP Signavio provides interplay with SAP Solution Manager and SAP Cloud ALM, alongside end-to-end governance of collaborative process redesign with interconnected process models, further integrated into enterprise architecture effects of process change with LeanIX.
