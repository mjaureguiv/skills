### ROW 133: Advanced analytics
**ANSWER:** SAP Signavio Process Intelligence and SAP Signavio Process Insights provide comprehensive analytical and data-based insights from various systems. At SAP Signavio we are investing on advanced analytics capabilities for our customers:
-Predictive analytics: Our automated insight feature includes time series analysis capabilities that allows for extrapolation-based anomaly and trend detection. As part of SAP Signavio Labs we also offer a prediction model. We plan to add it to our main product license in 2023.
- Prescriptive analytics: SAP Signavio Process Insights generates insights directly from enterprise system data and infers action and action plan recommendations from them, based on process performance benchmarking, hence facilitating the improvement of process inefficiencies. - Insight Analyzer (as part of SAP Signavio LabsI helps to provide context to an insight and the characteristics of the related cases. The analysis highlights the common attributes that have a higher correlation with high metric values, and allows a visual exploration of the different cases that make up an insight, and how they branch out in clusters, relative to the metric and their attributes.

## SECTION: 1. How does the company's Digital Twin of an Organization Solutions support digital transformation initiatives for its customers.

### ROW 4: Our ​Digital Twins of an Organization Solutions provide the ability to an organization to have a great representation of its current state, operating model, IT construct, and relationship between business processes to best practices. These solutions enable successful digital transformations and operational excellence initiatives especially when complex systems of records are involved in such transformation. The notion of having a great grasp on your current state is what we call process observability.
 SAP Signavio supports process observability and makes it actionable.
-Build a virtual representation of any process, with “to-be” models and “as-is” monitoring
-Model their processes to design optimum flows and maximum efficiency leveraging best practices and value accelerators
-Visualize process adjustments, based on one common collaborative platform
-Simulate different scenarios and calculate the impact of alternative measures
-Monitor the way their operations actually work - fact-based and objectively
-Identify effort drivers, reasons for delay, and potential for automation
-Create tangible impact with transparency and continuous process improvements and recommendations


## SECTION: 2. How does the company's Digital Twin of an Organization Solutions helps organizations to achieve operational excellence and customer service initiatives?

### ROW 9: We consider the best way to support operational excellence and customer service initiatives is a single, combined approach.
Our interpretation of digital twin does not stop with the operational reality, but considers as well the people experience of customers, employees, suppliers, or other stakeholders that interact with an organization´s business processes. 
On one side, our process mining offering with SAP Signavio Process Insights and SAP Signavio Process Intelligence helps organizations gain transparency into their as-is processes and assess how their operations actually run to foster process improvements. They can do this by comparing  as-is processes to their to-be processes, identifying issues and improvement opportunities in a continuous, governed cycle. By introducing a data-driven approach, organizations can become more efficient and effective with their processes, activities, people, and resources in business transformations and process excellence initiatives.
On top of this, we add an outside-in perspective to process excellence and transformation efforts by offering a combined view of an organization´s process and the experience reality. We achieve this by merging and analyzing process data from operational systems and experience management solutions and applying process mining techniques to highlight the relationship between experience and the operational reality. With this, process variants or behaviors that strongly correlate to high or low experience satisfaction scores can be rapidly identified.
This data can be displayed in the context of journey models (such as a customer journey) in our solution SAP Signavio Journey Modeler. The solution provides the ability - just like a digital twin - to represent in the relationship between journey steps and experiences to the operational reality - be business process, IT systems, tasks and teams in a visual way. Organizations can start modeling journey models to visualize the end-to-end experience that their customers, employees, or suppliers have with them, including journey steps and the underlying processes. On top of this, they can visualize the complexity (complexity scores) of each journey, an aspect which is fundamental as managing complexity is one of the key objectives of DTO. 
This allows to track the interactions of stakeholders (for instance customers) with the organization´s processes, and also improve based on their feedback.

### ROW 29: Yes, we provide a continuum from measuring KPIs and doing a quick scan of a company performance for SAP systems (SAP Signavio Process Insights) all the way to detailed process mining for end-to-end processes for SAP and non-SAP systems (SAP Signavio Process Intelligence). They do so by allowing organizations to easily connect all the needed process data, by jump-starting process mining projects with ready-to-use business content, and by quickly discovering areas for process improvement. At the same time, we offer the possibliity to combine the outside-in and inside-out perspectives to translate how people, meaning customers, employees and suppliers interact with an organization´s process and services and focus on delivering a better experience.
We also offer integration to task mining partner solutions to provide analyses of fine-grained user tasks, within process activities, allowing to analyse user behaviours.
When it comes to adapting and implementing changes in the organization´s processes, SAP Signavio Process Insights and SAP Signavio Process Intelligence are also instrumental in allowing organizations to get more quickly from insight to actual process change. They do so by providing ready-to-use recommendations, as well as a seamless integration with the rest of the SAP Signavio Process Transformation Suite, and integration touch points with automation engines.
With our new capability "actions" (curently in beta release , to be GA in Q2/2023) we're handing off the insights and the recommendations based on process mining technology for systems of record and automation software to improve the processes.
SAP Signavio Process Transformation has a unique span, supporting two complementary approaches:
 - fast process enhancements, with the tight integration of process mining (SAP Signavio Process Intelligence) and process automation (integration to SAP Build Process Automation and to other 3rd party tools)
 - full-blown process transformations, with the tight integration of process mining and process modelling, that allows to streamline the mining-to-modelling and modelling-to-deploy loop. 
We believe that this dual approach, enabling both fast and deep transformations, is unique in the market.
Concerning the fast approach, our distinctive choice was to provide a full end-to-end solution, that covers mining and automation, but to do it in a way that is still modular and flexible enough to remain open for our customers.
