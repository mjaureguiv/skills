# Sample Responses: Roadmap Session Requests

Examples of good responses to customer roadmap/strategy session requests.

---

## Example 1: Accepting with Additional Topics

**Context:** Customer requested SPG roadmap session, internal team wants to add important topics

**Response:**
```
Subject: Re: SPG Road Map & Strategy Session @[Customer]

Hello [Name],

I will be happy to help with the Product presentation of the roadmap. @[PM Name] - maybe you could help with the current product situation and developments?

[Name], I would also like to bring one more topic - [Topic Name].
[Brief explanation of why this is important for the customer]

Could we add this topic to the agenda?
@[Expert Name] - our [Role] will be able to answer the questions, and we could plan next steps with the customer.

A few questions to help us prepare:
1. When would you like to schedule this session?
2. How much time should we plan for? (We typically suggest 60-90 minutes)
3. Who will be joining from your side?

Thank you.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

---

## Example 2: Simple Confirmation

**Context:** Straightforward roadmap request, no additional topics

**Response:**
```
Subject: Re: SPG Roadmap Session Request

Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out! We'd be happy to do an SPG Roadmap & Strategy session with [Customer].

We can cover:
- Current SPG capabilities and recent updates
- Planned features and roadmap preview
- Q&A specific to your use cases

When would work best for your team? Please share a few options and we'll find a slot that works.

Looking forward to it!

Best regards,
[Name]
```

---

## Example 3: With Preparation Questions

**Context:** Need more info to prepare properly

**Response:**
```
Subject: Re: SPG Strategy Session

Hi [Name],

Great to hear you're interested in diving deeper into SPG! We'd love to set up a roadmap session.

To make sure we cover what's most relevant for [Customer], a few questions:

1. **Timing:** When would you like to schedule this? Any dates to avoid?
2. **Topics:** Are there specific areas you'd like us to focus on?
   - Workflow automation
   - Integrations
   - Reporting & analytics
   - User management
   - Other: ___
3. **Attendees:** Who will be joining? (Helps us tailor the technical depth)
4. **Duration:** 60 or 90 minutes?

Once I have these details, I'll coordinate with our product team and send a calendar invite.

Thanks!
[Name]
```

---

## Example 4: Reference to Similar Past Session

**Context:** Customer mentions a similar session they heard about

**Response:**
```
Subject: Re: SPG Roadmap Session (similar to [Other Customer])

Hi [Name],

Yes, we can definitely do a session similar to the one we did with [Other Customer]!

That session covered:
- SPG current state and recent enhancements
- Roadmap preview (Q2-Q4 outlook)
- Customer-specific use case discussion
- Q&A

We can customize the agenda based on [Customer]'s specific interests.

What timeframe works for scheduling? And any particular topics you'd like to prioritize?

Best regards,
[Name]
```

---

## Key Elements to Include

1. **Confirm willingness** - Yes, we can do this
2. **Propose additional relevant topics** (if any)
3. **Tag relevant internal experts**
4. **Ask for scheduling preferences**
5. **Ask about attendees** (helps tailor depth)
6. **Keep it friendly but professional**
