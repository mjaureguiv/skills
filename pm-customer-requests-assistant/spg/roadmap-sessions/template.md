## Pre-Session Prep

### Customer Research
- [ ] Review customer's current SPG setup
- [ ] Check their support ticket history
- [ ] Review any previous session notes
- [ ] Understand their industry/use cases

### Materials
- [ ] Prepare roadmap slides (current state)
- [ ] Prepare roadmap slides (planned features)
- [ ] Customize examples for their industry
- [ ] Prepare demo environment (if needed)

### Q&A Preparation
- [ ] Review likely questions based on their setup
- [ ] Prepare answers for common roadmap questions
- [ ] Note any features they've previously requested

### Logistics
- [ ] Confirm date/time with customer
- [ ] Send calendar invite
- [ ] Test screen sharing / meeting tech
- [ ] Schedule internal prep meeting

---

## Session Agenda (Suggested)

1. **Introductions** (5 min)
2. **Current SPG Overview** (10 min)
   - What's in the product today
   - Recent releases
3. **Roadmap Preview** (20 min)
   - Planned features
   - Timeline (quarters, not specific dates)
4. **Customer-Specific Discussion** (15 min)
   - Their use cases
   - Their challenges
5. **Q&A** (10 min)
