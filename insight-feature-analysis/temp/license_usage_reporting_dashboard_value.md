# License Usage Reporting Dashboard

## Capability
A comprehensive dashboard that enables administrators and stakeholders to:
- View real-time and historical license usage across the organization
- Export user, license, and group data (including license type, assignment date, last login, and status)
- Filter and segment data by user, license type, group, and time period
- Schedule automated reports for regular delivery (e.g., monthly)
- Identify unassigned, unused, or expired licenses
- Track license assignment changes and trends over time
- Differentiate between license categories (e.g., accelerator vs. collaborator)

## Benefits
- Saves significant manual effort for admins and support teams by automating reporting and exports
- Reduces risk of GDPR or compliance issues by providing auditable, up-to-date records
- Enables proactive license optimization, reducing wasted spend on unused licenses
- Improves transparency for stakeholders and supports better decision-making
- Simplifies audits and contract compliance by providing clear, exportable records
- Enhances user and license management efficiency, reducing errors and delays

## Product Value Proposition
Empower your organization with full visibility and control over SAP Signavio license usage. The License Usage Reporting Dashboard transforms manual, error-prone reporting into a seamless, automated process—freeing up admin time, reducing compliance risk, and ensuring every license delivers value. With powerful export, scheduling, and segmentation features, you can optimize license allocation, support audits, and drive adoption with confidence.
