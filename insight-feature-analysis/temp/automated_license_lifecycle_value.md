Automated License Lifecycle Management

Value Proposition
Optimize license allocation and reduce manual workload by automating the assignment, revocation, and renewal of licenses based on user activity and business rules. Ensure licenses are always allocated to active, relevant users, maximizing value and minimizing waste.
Benefits
1. Reduces administrative burden by automating repetitive license management tasks.
2. Improves license utilization and cost efficiency by reclaiming unused licenses promptly.
3. Enhances compliance and auditability through consistent, rule-based license management.
Capabilities
1. Automated workflows for assigning and revoking licenses based on user activity, role, or custom triggers.
2. Scheduled license reviews and notifications for inactive or underutilized accounts.
3. Integration with user management APIs and external systems for seamless lifecycle management.
