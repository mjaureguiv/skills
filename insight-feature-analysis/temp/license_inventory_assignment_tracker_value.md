License Inventory & Assignment Tracker

**Value Proposition**
Gain complete visibility and control over license assignments, ensuring every license is tracked, properly allocated, and aligned with organizational needs. Simplify license management and reduce confusion by providing clear, actionable information about license inventory and user entitlements.
**Benefits**
Prevents license waste and over-allocation by tracking assignments and availability in real time.
Reduces confusion and errors by clarifying license types, entitlements, and assignment status.
Streamlines audits and compliance by maintaining accurate, up-to-date records of license distribution.
**Capabilities**
Real-time dashboard showing assigned, available, and pending licenses by type and user.
Tools for bulk assignment, reassignment, and removal of licenses with clear audit trails.
Contextual explanations and guidance for admins on license types, entitlements, and assignment best practices.
