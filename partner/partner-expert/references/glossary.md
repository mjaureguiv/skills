# Partner Glossary

> **Source**: SAP BBP Strategy PPT + PE Program Guide + PES ISV Summary
> **Last updated**: 2026-03-27

---

## Abbreviations

| Abbreviation | Full Form | Context |
|---|---|---|
| B/B/P | Build/Buy/Partner | SAP's strategic decision framework for portfolio gaps |
| BBP | Build/Buy/Partner | Same as B/B/P |
| BD | Business Development Lead | PES role managing commercial partner relationships |
| BMP | Business Model Pricing Team | Handles pricing models for partner solutions |
| BTP | Business Technology Platform | SAP's platform for extensions and integrations |
| CPO | Chief Product Officer | Owns overall product strategy |
| CRO | Chief Revenue Officer | Sales leadership |
| CMSO | Chief Marketing & Solutions Officer | Marketing leadership |
| EA | Endorsed App | SAP's preferred partner apps program |
| E2E | End-to-End | Complete process from start to finish |
| GSSP | Global Strategic Service Partner | Large SI firms |
| GTM | Go-to-Market | Sales and marketing execution strategy |
| ICC | Integration & Certification Center | Manages technical certifications for partner solutions |
| IP | Intellectual Property | Ownership of technology and solution rights |
| ISV | Independent Software Vendor | Software company that partners with SAP |
| LoB | Line of Business | SAP's organizational divisions (HCM, CX, SCM, etc.) |
| M&A | Merger & Acquisition | Buying or merging with another company |
| MU | Market Unit | Regional sales unit at SAP |
| OEM | Original Equipment Manufacturer | Licensing model (Inbound = partner into SAP, Outbound = SAP into partner) |
| PBM | Partner Business Manager | PES role managing day-to-day partner relationship |
| PE | PartnerEdge | SAP's partner program |
| PE Build | PartnerEdge Build | Program for ISVs building on SAP technology |
| PES | Partner Ecosystem Success | SAP organization managing partner relationships |
| PPWG | Partner Portfolio Working Group | Monthly governance forum for reviewing partner nominations |
| PQ | Premium Qualification | Certification process for Solution Extensions |
| PRM | Partner Relationship Management | System for managing partner data |
| ROI | Return on Investment | Financial return metric |
| SAL | Solution Area Lead | PES role leading a solution domain |
| SIX | Solution & Innovation Experience | Brings industry expertise, demos, and enablement |
| SolEx | Solution Extension | Partner solution sold on SAP paper |
| STCA | Software Technology Cooperation Agreement | Legal agreement for tech collaboration |
| SDCA | Software Development Cooperation Agreement | Legal agreement for joint development projects |
| SPDA | Software Partner Development Agreement | Legal agreement for partner development |
| TPT | Test, Provisioning & Tenant | Cloud delivery setup for partner solutions |
| 3iaB | Three-in-a-Box | Approval body (Product, Sales, Partner) for nominations |
| ACV | Annual Contract Value | Yearly value of a cloud subscription contract |
| ARC | Application Readiness Check | Technical assessment a solution must pass to become GTM-ready on SAP Store |
| CEE | Customer Engagement Executive | Mandatory partner role for Sell track product authorizations |
| COE | Center of Expertise | Partner certification for VAR-delivered support |
| PGA | Partner Grouping Agreement | Agreement allowing affiliates to pool competencies under one partner group |
| VAR | Value-Added Reseller | Partner that resells SAP solutions with added services |
| A&C | Adoption & Consumption | SAP process for driving customer usage of deployed solutions |
| BDC | Business Data Cloud | SAP's data management platform |
| BTM | Business Transformation Management | Solution area that includes SAP Signavio |
| PC | Premium Certification | Highest certification level for partner solutions |
| TCV | Total Contract Value | Full value of a contract over its entire term |
| 19PP | 19 Percentage Points | Target for ISV-driven renewal rate improvement |

## Key Concepts

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Whitespace** | Gap in SAP's portfolio where no SAP or partner solution addresses a clear customer need |
| **Partner Space** | A whitespace designated for a partnering approach |
| **Clean Core** | SAP's mandate to keep core extensions cloud-native and upgrade-safe |
| **Business Capability Matrix** | Tool for mapping existing and planned solutions against customer needs to find gaps |
| **Partner Progression** | Movement from lower-tier (Store Listed) to higher-tier (Endorsed/SolEx) partner status |
| **Partner Regression** | Demotion from higher-tier to lower-tier due to underperformance or strategic misfit |
| **Premium Certification** | SAP's highest certification level for Endorsed Apps |
| **Premium Qualification** | SAP's internal validation process for Solution Extensions |
| **GTM-Ready** | Status of a partner solution that has passed the Application Readiness Check and is cleared for go-to-market |
| **Spotlight+** | Solution designation for high-performing SAP Store solutions meeting sales and BTP certification thresholds |
| **Safeguarding Period** | Grace period (90-200 days depending on context) before a partner loses a designation, level, or competency |
| **Competency Designation** | Recognition of a partner's solution-specific skills across three tiers: Essential, Advanced, Expert |
| **Specialization** | Product-level capability designation — building blocks toward a competency |
| **Partner Current Cloud Backlog** | Contractually committed cloud subscription revenue SAP expects to recognize over the next 12 months |
| **Inbound OEM** | Partner solution is embedded into an SAP product |
| **Outbound OEM** | SAP technology is packaged into a partner's product |
| **Application Readiness Check (ARC)** | Technical assessment before Store listing |
