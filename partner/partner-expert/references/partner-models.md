## SAP PartnerEdge Program Overview

The SAP PartnerEdge program is SAP's partner ecosystem framework. It has **four tracks** that define how partners collaborate with SAP:

| Track | Who It's For | Fee (Annual) | Notes |
|-------|-------------|--------------|-------|
| **Build** | Partners that design, develop, and commercialize extensions, integrations, and applications for SAP solutions | €2,000 | Go-to-market via SAP Store |
| **Sell** | Partners that resell SAP solutions (end-to-end customer lifecycle) | €3,700 | Requires product authorization |
| **Service** | Partners providing business consulting, implementation, and integration services | €3,700 | Requires delivery capability |
| **Run** | Partners providing fully managed services with SAP solutions | Invitation only, no fee | By invitation only |

A partner can participate in multiple tracks. Build + any other track = €3,700 combined.

## Partner Levels (Recognition Framework)

Four progressive levels based on points earned across performance categories:

| Level | Description | How to Reach |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **SAP Partner** | Base level, foundational benefits | Meet minimum program requirements |
| **SAP Silver Partner** | Demonstrated expertise in cloud delivery | Earn enough points (threshold TBD in portal) |
| **SAP Gold Partner** | Very close SAP alignment, exceptional capabilities | Higher point threshold, or GROW with SAP designation (auto-Gold at 500 pts) |
| **SAP Platinum Partner** | Highest level, outstanding global success | Invitation only — not dictated by points |

### Points System

| Category | Metric | Points |
|----------|--------|--------|
| **Sales Performance** | Partner current cloud backlog (size) | €5,000 = 1 point (max 300) |
| **Sales Performance** | Cloud backlog year-over-year growth | 3% growth = 2 points (min prev year €150K) |
| **Adoption Performance** | Partner-led deployed ACV (last 4 quarters) | €150K-399K = 150 pts; €400K-849K = 200 pts; ≥€850K = 250 pts (max 250) |
| **Delivery Performance** | Competency held | Essential = 100; Advanced = 125; Expert = 150 pts (max 250) |
| **Strategic Alignment** | GROW with SAP designation | 500 pts (auto SAP Gold) |

**Safeguarding**: If a partner falls below a level's threshold, they get a **200-day safeguarding period** to recover points before being downgraded.

## Solution Designations (Build Track Only)

These are the key designations for ISV partner solutions — the progression path that matters most for Signavio's partner strategy:

### Progression Path

```
SAP Store Listed → Spotlight+ → SAP Endorsed App → Solution Extension (SolEx)
```

### SAP Store Listed
- **What**: Partner solution listed on SAP Store marketplace
- **Prerequisites**: GTM-ready status (passed Application Readiness Check), valid Build track membership
- **Revenue share**: Must pay revenue share to SAP per Build track price list, regardless of who facilitated the sale
- **Certification**: Optional but recommended

### Spotlight+
- **What**: Enhanced visibility and co-sell support for high-performing Store solutions
- **How to attain**: Automatically evaluated at the start of every calendar quarter
- **Requirements**:
  - **Sales performance**: New and up-sell bookings (average contract value) on SAP Store ≥ €150,000 in prior 4 quarters
  - **Strategic alignment**: Must hold valid SAP BTP certification in an eligible scenario (BTP-APP, BTP-EXT, BTP-EXT-CC, BTP-EXT-GENAI, BTP-EXT-S/4PUB, etc.)
- **Safeguarding**: 3-quarter safeguarding period if sales dip below threshold (strategic alignment must still be met)

### SAP Endorsed Apps
- **What**: SAP's preferred partner apps — deep alignment with SAP roadmap, GTM motions, and customer trust
- **How to attain**: **Invitation only** — cannot be self-nominated
- **Requirements**: Solution must support SAP's strategic direction, address a significant market opportunity or customer need, and cover a white space in the SAP portfolio
- **Certification**: Premium Certification required (SAP's highest level)
- **See also**: `bbp-framework.md` → "Partner Solution Progression" for the full value/volume framework

### Solution Extensions (SolEx)
- **What**: Third-party solutions sold by SAP and SAP partners on SAP paper
- **How to attain**: **Invitation only** — requires PPWG approval and premium qualification
- **Key difference from Endorsed**: SAP takes full resell/liability responsibility. Solution is sold as if it were SAP's own.
- **Certification**: Premium Qualification (PQ) required — 4-6 week validation process
- **Sales/implementation rules**: Set by the third-party vendor, must be adhered to by Sell/Service track partners

## Program Requirements

### Build Track Requirements
1. **GTM-ready solution**: At least one solution must pass partner solution review (Application Readiness Check)
2. **SAP Application Interface Register**: Any solution integrating via SAP APIs must use the unique integration key
3. **Revenue share**: Mandatory on all transactions (new, up-sell, renewal)
4. **Development license**: Available for developing on SAP platforms, cloud services, APIs

### Sell Track Requirements
1. **Product authorization**: Must attain per-product authorization with certified consultants (CEE, Sales Exec, Presales, Solution Consultant)
2. **Compliance training**: Two executives must complete online compliance training (valid 2 years)
3. **Delivery capability**: Must hold a competency designation or maintain minimum certified consultants
4. **Business plan**: Annual submission required, including cloud-targeted ACV commitment
5. **Support authorization**: If providing on-prem VAR support, must have Partner COE certification

### Service Track Requirements
1. **Delivery capability**: Competency designation required, or minimum certified consultants
2. **Project registration**: Must register customer projects and waves in SAP for Me

## Competencies & Specializations

Partners earn competency designations across three progressive tiers:

| Tier | Level | Requirements Pattern |
|------|-------|---------------------|
| **Essential** | Entry-level competency | ~3 solution consultants + 1 project manager + 1 delivered project |
| **Advanced** | Mid-level | ~5 consultants + 2 PMs + 3 projects + platform experts |
| **Expert** | Top-level | ~10 consultants + 3 PMs + 5 projects + partner solution on BTP |

### Signavio-Relevant Competencies

The **Business Transformation Management** competency includes specializations directly relevant to Signavio:

| Specialization | Solution Consultants (Essential → Advanced → Expert) |
|---|---|
| **Business Process Management (SAP Signavio)** | 3 → 5 → 10 |
| **Process Analysis and Mining (SAP Signavio)** | 3 → 5 → 10 |
| **Enterprise Architecture (SAP LeanIX)** | 3 → 5 → 10 |
| **Digital Adoption (WalkMe)** | 3 → 5 → 10 |

**90-day safeguarding**: If a competency requirement dips, partners get 90 days to fix it before downgrade/loss.

## Customer Success Metrics

Partners are held accountable to customer success KPIs (traffic light system):

| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| **Waves created in SAP for Me** (within 90 days of contract start) | >90% | 50-90% | <50% |
| **Wave health indicator** (during implementation) | <1.5% escalation | 1.5-2% | >2% |
